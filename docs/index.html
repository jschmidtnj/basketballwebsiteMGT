About





Overview


Overall: Newton High School is a fun place to play basketball because of our gym and the support we get from the community. The Boys Basketball program has a proud tradition as a leader in West Jersey hoops. NHS teams have won 3 SCIL championships in the late 90's, we were the 2016 Colonial Division Championship, 2017 + 2018 Freedom Division Championships. We are very competitive in our section as well. NHS has played in 8 Group 2 Section 1 Finals winning a Group 2 Section 1 Title in 2017. Newton Boys Basketball is one of only 3 schools in the last 50 years to win a NJSIAA Group 2 sectional title from Sussex County. The overall record at Newton High School the last 7 years is 124-54. Our top players go on to play in college where we have or had 8 players in college at the D-2, D-3 or Junior College levels in the last 7 years.

Mission Statement: The mission of the Boys Basketball Program is that the young men learn to be leaders in the classroom and on the court. We believe that these skills are more important than the game and that the game should be learned to teach life lessons. The players are expected to practice every day, work hard at practice, learn to become part of a team, play hard during games, exhibit good sportsmanship and learn to communicate with the coaches and their teammates. We believe that the life skills that are learned being part of the basketball team, will aid each student to become successful when they graduate Newton High School.

Season: The season runs from November to the middle of March. They will play a 22 game schedule and they will play in a holiday tournament, HWS Tournament and the NJSIAA state tournament. Please plan to be home during the Christmas Break because the young men will have practices and games.

Off Season: From March to June the players play other sports or AAU or concentrate on their school work or other activities. In June the summer season starts. We play summer leagues in Sparta, Hackensack, and go to team camp. We also hold open gyms in June and July. In September we start up again with the available players in a Fall League.





Coaches



Head Coach Dirk Kelly



Coach Kelly has over 23 years of varsity experience. He was the Varsity Basketball Coach at Newton High School from 1992-2000 and returned in 2011. Over that period his teams won 3 SCIL championships, 1 Colonial Division, 2 Freedom Division Championships, and played in 6 Group 2 Section 1 Finals winning a Group 2 Section 1 Title in 2017. The overall record at Newton High School the last 7 years is 124-54. 7 players have gone on to play in college at the D-2, D-3 or Junior College levels during that time span.

Newton High School basketball teams were named Team of the Year in 1996 NJ Herald, 1998 and 1999 Star Ledger and NJ Herald.

Coach Kelly has also coached at Morris Knolls High School, Roxbury High School and Sussex County Community College. During those coaching stints he has over 330 wins. He was named Coach of the Year by the Star Ledger in 1995, NJ Herald 1996 and 2017, The Daily Record 1998, Mugs Media, 2012, 2016, 2017, NJ.com NJAC Coach of the Year 2018.

Coach Kelly played at FDU-Madison for four years where he was the captain for 2.

Assistant Coach Paul Filan



Coach Filan has been a valuable assistant basketball coach at Newton High School for the last 12 years. He has coached a variety of levels from Freshman, JV and Varsity. He also is an assistant in the boys soccer program and the girls softball program. He is instrumental in teaching and developing Newton's talented players to move on to the next level.

Coach Filan played at NHS and was named MVP his Senior year. His team won the SCIL Festival and he was named MVP for the entire tournament.

Assistant Coach Ken Teets



Coach Teets is a volunteer assistant coach. He works behind the scenes with fundraising, video coordination, and team morale. He is a police officer in Newton and works with the team during his off hours. Ken has been working with the Newton High School students for the last 6 years. He is a valuable asset to the team.
